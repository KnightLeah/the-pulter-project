Pearled Over

by Victoria E. Burke

Pulter describes the goddess Aurora as “Pearl’d or’e with odours of the early East” (3). Here is an image of a seventeenth-century woman fashionably sporting pearls around her neck, in her ears, in her hair, and over her dress.

Detail from Anna Sophia, Countess of Carnarvon, by Anthony van Dyck. Public domain, via Wikimedia Commons.
